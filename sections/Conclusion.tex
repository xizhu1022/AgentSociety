\section{Conclusion}
This paper presents a blockchain-supported question answering ecosystem as a representative example of decentralized coordination. We integrate PoI validation with a tokenomics–knowledge dual loop, aligning user incentives through verifiable consensus.
Within a unified game-theoretic framework, we establish incentive compatibility of validators and individual rationality of consumers and producers, proving the existence of a symmetric Bayesian–Nash equilibrium that sustains rational participation and stable matching.
As a proof-of-concept, this work demonstrates a tangible step toward building next-generation decentralized internet applications of people, for people, and by people.
